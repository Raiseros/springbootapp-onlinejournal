St. Petersburg international salon of education 2020

26 April 2020


In 2020, as part of the St. Petersburg international salon of education, the St. Petersburg education Committee will take part in the MMSO 2020 exhibition in the Public policy cluster.

The stand will be organized by:

  • exhibition area (for presentation by educational institutions of joint educational projects, including methodological developments created by St. Petersburg manufacturers, as well as their social partners, as well as for conducting master classes);


  • conference area (for events in various formats (experience presentations, case studies, practice presentations, panel discussions, round tables, master classes) with representatives of the St. Petersburg education system);


  • lounge area (for free communication between representatives of the St. Petersburg education system and regional delegations);


  • meeting room (for the heads of the Committee and educational institutions to meet with MSO exhibitors).
