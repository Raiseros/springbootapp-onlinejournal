Dear parents and legal representatives!

13 July 2020


In connection with the cancellation of a number of restrictive measures related to the introduction of a high-alert regime on the territory of St. Petersburg, in order to further ensure the sanitary and epidemiological well-being of the population, the education Committee will resume personal reception of citizens and acceptance of applications from July 13.

Attention of parents and legal representatives! July 13 is the last day when an application to the Commission for the settlement of disputes in the implementation of the right to receive General education in St. Petersburg can be submitted electronically through the portal "Petersburg education". Further, starting from July 20, applications will be accepted at the education Committee (Antonenko lane, 8, room 343) on Mondays from 9.30 to 12.00. We remind you that the Commission considers applications from parents when they receive a refusal to enter the first classes of educational institutions under the jurisdiction of the education Committee (Academic gymnasium No. 56 of St. Petersburg, Second St. Petersburg Gymnasium, Engineering and technology No. 777 of St. Petersburg).

In order to prevent the risks of infection with a new coronavirus infection (COVID-19), applicants using personal respiratory and hand protection equipment are allowed to the education Committee, with a social distance of at least 1.5 meters.
