There are two days left before children start enrolling in the first classes

12 December 2019


On Sunday, December 15, the first stage of enrollment of children in the first classes for the 2020/2021 academic year starts. Participants of a press conference held today in the media center of the city Government reminded the residents of St. Petersburg about the procedure for submitting applications.
