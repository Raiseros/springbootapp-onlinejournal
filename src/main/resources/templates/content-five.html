The September period of State final certification has started in St. Petersburg

3 September 2019


An additional period of State final certification has started. Today, September 3, graduates of the 9th and 11th grades of the city's schools passed the Russian language exams. To pass the test, children need to score at least 36 points.

During the September period, the GIA registered 262 eleventh-graders and 1,161 ninth-graders. The final wave is attended by students who did not pass the certification in the main and reserve terms for a good reason or received an unsatisfactory result earlier. Exams will be held both on the basis of General education institutions in the city and at home. This opportunity is provided for children with disabilities.
