Electronic journal of a student allows you to monitor school performance, track implementation of the school curriculum, as well as ensure that all interested parties are informed in a timely manner about the progress and results of the educational process. To access the electronic journal, you need to log in to Your personal account enter or check the correctness of your data: full name, e-mail, and class, and also contact the school and inform their class teacher. Important! The information in the school and personal account must match.
