About the journal


SES electronic magazine and diary is one of the key services of the Saint-Petersburg electronic school project. It combines advanced innovative solutions and familiar technical tools.

The electronic journal and diary of SES helps to ensure a high level of quality of education in every school, in every class, in every lesson. With the help of the SES diary and journal, the school administration effectively manages the educational process, determines the certification periods, forms of intermediate certification, and also provides automatic generation of reports on academic performance. Teachers form a calendar and thematic plan and make time-based planning, distribute content elements in lessons, link them to textbooks and lesson scenarios, and conduct intermediate certification. Parents also have full information about the progress and results of their children's education.

The SES diary and journal is also a communication tool between the class teacher and parents. The educational process becomes transparent for all participants – parents, teachers, and students. The electronic diary and magazine, as well as the SES Library, are educational services of the Saint-Petersburg Electronic School – a modern school of the XXI century that provides a high level of quality education to every student and provides new modern tools to every teacher in Saint Petersburg.
