Presentation of an interactive tour dedicated to the 75th anniversary of the Palace of students of St. Petersburg

7 October 2019


On October 7, at 12:00, the Museum of the history of professional education of the Palace of students of St. Petersburg (64 Sinopskaya nab.) will present an interactive tour dedicated to the 75th anniversary of the Palace of students of St. Petersburg.

The interactive tour will be devoted to the history of the Palace of students of St. Petersburg, the creation of the first creative teams, and the professional development of the institution.

The interactive tour will be included in the program of the IX Forum of Small museums.

The forum of small museums is an annual autumn festival where dozens of "small" museums hold interesting programs and events for residents and visitors of the city. Among the museums participating in the forum are regional, industrial, departmental museums, exhibition sites of libraries and even Church parishes.

The Forum is organized by the Citywide scientific and methodological Center for the theory and practice of Museum business on the basis of the Saint Petersburg state LIBRARY "Raznochinny Petersburg Museum".
